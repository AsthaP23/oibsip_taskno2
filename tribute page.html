APJ Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Biography

APJ Abdul Kalam's full name was "Abul Pakir Zainul Abdin Abdul Kalam Masoudi". He was also known as Missile Man and the President of the people. He was also honored with 'Padma Bhushan' and 'Bharat Ratna'. He was appointed the eleventh President of India in 2002. He served as President from 2002 to 2007 and contributed to the development of the country. On 27 July 2015, he died due to a heart attack. He was cremated with full military honours. Dr. Abdul Kalam was a great scientist. He was also the Vice Chancellor of the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology. He successfully completed many space programs of ISRO.
